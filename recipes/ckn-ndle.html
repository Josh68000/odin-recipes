Chicken Noodles

Description

A quick and easy chicken noodles recipe.

Every bite is like having a bite of chicken noodles

Ingredients

  • 1 deboned chicken thigh
  • 1 pack of Ko-Lee hot and spicy noodles
  • 100g frozen peas
  • 100g frozen sweetcorn

Recipe

  1. Dice thigs into 1cm chunks, then put in airfryer for 10 mins, turning regularly
  2. Pour frozen peas and sweetcorn into saucepan, boil for 5 mins
  3. Put noodles into sauce pan, and pour over 250ml of boiling water
  4. Once veg cooked, drain and transfer to noodle pan
  5. Once chicken thigh cooked, also add to noodle pan
  6. Serve and enjoy
