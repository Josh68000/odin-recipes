Curry

Description

A tasty and easy to make curry. Perfect replacement for a takeaway.

Throw a naan in, live a little.

Ingredients

  • 1 tin coconut milk
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 2 chicken thighs, deboned
  • 1 red onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp Bart pilau mix
  • 140g rice

Recipe

  1. Rinse rice, until water runs clear. Drain, and set aside
  2. Peel and dice onion, set to side
  3. Peel and dice garlic, set to side
  4. Fry onion on medium heat until translucent.
  5. Once onion is translucent, add garlic fry for 30 seconds then add chicken thigh
  6. Tranfer rice to lidded saucepan, add pilau mix
  7. Pour 250ml of cold water over rice
  8. Put rice on high heat, until water starts to boil. Once boiling, cover, and turn heat to low
    • Do not take lid off rice for 10 mins
  9. Add chopped tomatoes and coconut milk to frying pan, and let simmmer for 15 mins
  10. Once rice has cooked for 10 mins, transfer to sauce pan, cover and leave for 10 mins
  11. Enjoy!
