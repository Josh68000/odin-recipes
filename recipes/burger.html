Burger

Description

Make your own patties with this new delicious burger recipe.

Just like a Big Mac but healthier, cheaper, and tastier.

Ingredients per 1 burger

For the patty:

  • 100g beef mince
  • 1 tsp rosemary
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp oregano

For the sauce:

  • 1 tsp mayo
  • 1/2 tsp sweet piccalilli
  • 1/8 tsp white vinegar
  • Pinch of white pepper
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powerder

For the burger:

  • 1 cup lettuce
  • 1/2 white onion
  • 1 gherkin
  • 1 brioche bun
  • 1 slice American cheese

Recipe

  1. Mix all ingredients for sauce together, and set aside
  2. Put 100g of mince into a mixing bowl, and herbs / spices and fold until smooth
  3. Dice half onion and fry half, set half aside
  4. Air fry for 5 mins, flip burger, air fry for 5 more mins
  5. Once patty has been air fried for 10 mins, put the slice of American cheese on top and leave for 2 mins
  6. Put bun in toaster for 2 mins
  7. Take buns out of toaster and arrange as per below
    1. Top bun
    2. Gherkin
    3. American cheese
    4. Patty
    5. Onion
    6. Lettuce
    7. Sauce
    8. Bottom bun
  8. Enjoy! :)
